BB Tracker — Installation
═════════════════════════

STEP 1 — Install
────────────────
Drag "BB Tracker" into your Applications folder.


STEP 2 — IMPORTANT: First launch
────────────────────────────────
BB Tracker is not yet notarized by Apple, so macOS will block
the first launch. You only need to do this once.

   ⚠  DO NOT just double-click the app — macOS will say
      "BB Tracker is damaged" or "cannot be opened".

   ✓  Instead, RIGHT-CLICK "BB Tracker" in Applications,
      choose "Open", then click "Open" in the dialog.

If macOS still blocks it (common on macOS Sequoia / 15+):

   1. Try to open it once (it will be blocked).
   2. Open System Settings → Privacy & Security.
   3. Scroll down to the Security section.
   4. You'll see: "BB Tracker was blocked from use".
   5. Click "Open Anyway".
   6. Confirm with your Mac password.


STEP 3 — Use it
───────────────
BB Tracker lives in your menu bar (top-right, near the clock).
Click the icon to see your grades and attendance.

On first launch, a window will guide you through signing
in to Blackboard. You only do this once.
